About Carnival Utopia

Tour operator Carnival Utopia started its activities in 2013. Carnival Utopia was founded by sworn traveler Tadas Kertenis, who decided to share his knowledge with travel fans on how to choose the best option for a flight and other necessary services; how to take care of all travel arrangements.

Since its establishment, Carnival Utopia has been accompanied by the slogan: "Plan easily". We are convinced that Carnival Utopia services must be such that our own team, family and relatives would definitely choose.

All the worries of holiday planning can be solved with a few clicks of a button - this is the mission of Carnival Utopia. It is important for our team not only to offer cheap travel packages, but also to take care of their quality: to choose good hotels, comfortable and reliable airlines.

We value our customers and their time, so we do our best to make them happy with their chosen trip, so that only the best impressions accompany travelers in the trip offered by Carnival Utopia.

We are constantly growing and improving, using and developing new technologies and gradually changing the habits of travelers. How? We have simplified the whole travel purchase process down to the smallest detail - the customer does not have to hesitate or doubt: Carnival Utopia systematizes and provides the best, cheapest and most convenient travel offers.

We are constantly looking for and offering the best travel, plane tickets, hotel prices for those who want to travel, and we provide services of high quality and at an affordable price. Those who want to see the world do not have to bother - planning a dream trip with Carnival Utopia is easy.

During its period of operation, Carnival Utopia has gathered a large circle of partners in Lithuania and abroad, so it can offer customers better prices, more flexible payment terms and, of course, the opportunity to book trips.
